Prologue
The King Who Reached Too Far

Three hundred years before your birth, King Aldros Vael ruled the island of Ashenmere from the Shattered Spire — a tower so tall it pierced the clouds above the Void Wastes.

He was not a cruel king. He was a frightened one. Terrified of death, he commissioned his scholars to find a path to immortality through the void — the ancient darkness that underlies all things.

They found it. They told him it would cost everything. He paid it anyway.

— —
The Ritual
The Night of the Shattering

On the night of the dark moon, Aldros performed the Rite of Unbinding at the Void Altar in the heart of the Wastes. Seven shards of crystallised void energy. Seven sacrifices. One king who wanted to live forever.

The void answered. But not with the gift he asked for.

The rift tore open. The island cracked from coast to coast. His body dissolved — not into death, but into the void itself. Aldros Vael became the Void Entity. Something vast. Something hungry. Something that remembered being human, and hated that it did.

— —
The Present
The Awakened

The rift never fully closed. For three centuries it has bled void energy into Ashenmere — corrupting the land, twisting the creatures, driving the survivors into walled settlements at the coast while the interior became something else entirely.

And now a cult has formed in the darkness.

They call themselves The Awakened. They believe Aldros was not destroyed but imprisoned — that the rift is a cage, not a wound. They have spent decades hunting the seven Ritual Shards scattered across the island when the Shattering tore them apart.

They have found most of them. They intend to finish what Aldros started.

— —
Your Role
An Heir Rises

Your house has stood on this island for generations. Whatever your ancestors did — or failed to do — the void remembers. It always remembers.

You are not the first of your line to fight this darkness. You will not be the last. Death here is not an ending — it is a handoff. Your heir carries the legacy forward.

The corruption rises. The cult moves closer to completion. A woman in the Ashen Flagon knows things she will not yet say. And somewhere in the Void Wastes, the Entity waits — patient as the dark between stars.
